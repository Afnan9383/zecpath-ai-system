4. Shipping Assistant
Experience: 0–2 years
Salary (India): ₹2.0 LPA – ₹3.5 LPA
Job Description:
Assists in shipping operations including packaging, labeling, and shipment processing.
Key Responsibilities:
• Prepare shipping documents
• Coordinate with courier and freight services
• Label and package shipments
• Track shipping status
• Maintain shipment records
Skills Required:
• Knowledge of shipping procedures
• Attention to detail
• Basic computer skills
