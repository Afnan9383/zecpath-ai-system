27. Freight Operations Supervisor
Experience: 5–10 years
Salary (India): ₹5.5 LPA – ₹11.0 LPA
Job Description:
Manages freight operations including carrier coordination, shipment planning, and cost
optimization.
Key Responsibilities:
• Supervise freight booking and operations
• Negotiate with carriers and vendors
• Monitor shipment movement
• Manage freight costs and performance
• Handle claims and escalations
Skills Required:
• Freight forwarding expertise
• Negotiation skills
• Vendor management
