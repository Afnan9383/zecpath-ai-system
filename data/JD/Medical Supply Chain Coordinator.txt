78. Medical Supply Chain Coordinator
Experience: 2–6 years
Salary (India): ₹4.0 LPA – ₹9.0 LPA
Job Description:
Coordinates the supply chain of medical products, ensuring availability of critical supplies
such as medicines, devices, and hospital consumables.
Key Responsibilities:
• Manage procurement and distribution of medical supplies
• Coordinate with hospitals, suppliers, and distributors
• Track inventory levels and demand
• Ensure timely delivery of critical items
• Maintain regulatory compliance and documentation
• Handle emergency supply requirements
Skills Required:
• Healthcare supply chain knowledge
• Inventory and demand planning
• Coordination and communication skills
• Compliance awareness
