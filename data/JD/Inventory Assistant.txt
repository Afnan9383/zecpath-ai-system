6. Inventory Assistant
Experience: 0–2 years
Salary (India): ₹1.8 LPA – ₹3.2 LPA
Job Description:
Supports inventory management by tracking stock levels and ensuring data accuracy.
Key Responsibilities:
• Monitor stock levels
• Update inventory records
• Conduct stock audits and cycle counts
• Identify discrepancies
• Assist in inventory planning
Skills Required:
• Inventory management basics
• Excel skills
• Attention to detail
