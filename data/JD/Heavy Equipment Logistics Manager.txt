81. Heavy Equipment Logistics Manager
Experience: 8–15 years
Salary (India): ₹10.0 LPA – ₹25.0 LPA (higher for international roles)
Job Description:
Manages transportation and handling of heavy machinery and oversized cargo used in
construction, oil & gas, and heavy industries.
Key Responsibilities:
• Plan and execute transportation of heavy and oversized equipment
• Coordinate with transport agencies and crane operators

• Ensure compliance with safety and regulatory standards
• Optimize routes and transport methods for heavy cargo
• Manage logistics costs and budgets
• Supervise logistics teams and operations
Skills Required:
• Heavy lift and project cargo expertise
• Route survey and planning
• Safety and compliance knowledge
• Leadership and vendor management
