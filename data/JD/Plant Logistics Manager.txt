75. Plant Logistics Manager
Experience: 8–15 years
Salary (India): ₹10.0 LPA – ₹25.0 LPA
Job Description:
Oversees all logistics operations within a manufacturing plant, including inbound materials,
internal movement, and outbound dispatch.
Key Responsibilities:
• Manage end-to-end plant logistics operations
• Oversee inbound, in-plant, and outbound logistics
• Optimize material flow and storage within the plant
• Ensure inventory accuracy and control
• Implement lean logistics and process improvements
• Manage logistics teams and vendors
• Monitor KPIs such as turnaround time and cost efficiency
Skills Required:

• Manufacturing and plant logistics expertise
• Lean manufacturing and process optimization
• Leadership and team management
• ERP/WMS systems
