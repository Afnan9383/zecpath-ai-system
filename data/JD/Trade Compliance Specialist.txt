51. Trade Compliance Specialist
Experience: 4–10 years
Salary (India): ₹6.0 LPA – ₹14.0 LPA
Job Description:
Ensures that all international trade activities comply with local and global trade regulations,
laws, and company policies.
Key Responsibilities:
• Ensure compliance with import/export laws and regulations
• Monitor trade policies and regulatory changes
• Conduct compliance audits and risk assessments
• Manage export controls and restricted goods
• Train teams on compliance requirements
• Maintain documentation and compliance records
Skills Required:
• Trade compliance and regulatory knowledge
• Risk management and auditing
• Analytical and legal understanding
• Attention to detail
