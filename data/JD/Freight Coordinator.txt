12. Freight Coordinator
Experience: 2–5 years
Salary (India): ₹3.0 LPA – ₹6.5 LPA
Job Description:
Handles freight operations including booking shipments, negotiating rates, and coordinating
with carriers.
Key Responsibilities:
• Arrange freight bookings (air, sea, road)
• Negotiate freight rates with carriers
• Track shipments and ensure on-time delivery
• Manage freight documentation
• Handle freight claims and discrepancies
Skills Required:
• Freight forwarding knowledge
• Negotiation skills
• Knowledge of Incoterms
