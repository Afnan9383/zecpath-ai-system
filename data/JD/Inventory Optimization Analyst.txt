46. Inventory Optimization Analyst
Experience: 3–8 years
Salary (India): ₹5.0 LPA – ₹14.0 LPA
Job Description:
Analyzes inventory data to optimize stock levels, reduce carrying costs, and prevent
stockouts or overstocking.
Key Responsibilities:
• Analyze demand patterns and inventory trends
• Optimize safety stock and reorder levels
• Reduce excess and obsolete inventory
• Develop inventory models and forecasts
• Collaborate with procurement and logistics teams
• Generate inventory performance reports and insights
Skills Required:

• Data analysis and forecasting
• Advanced Excel, SQL, or analytics tools
• Inventory planning techniques (EOQ, safety stock)
• Problem-solving and analytical thinking
