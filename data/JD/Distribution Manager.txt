33. Distribution Manager
Experience: 8–15 years
Salary (India): ₹8.5 LPA – ₹18.0 LPA
Job Description:
Leads distribution operations ensuring efficient movement of goods from warehouses to
customers.
Key Responsibilities:
• Manage distribution networks and channels
• Optimize delivery schedules and routes
• Monitor service levels and customer satisfaction
• Coordinate with logistics and warehouse teams
• Improve distribution efficiency
Skills Required:
• Distribution planning
• Analytical and decision-making skills
• Coordination and leadership
