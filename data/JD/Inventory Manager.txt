37. Inventory Manager
Experience: 8–15 years
Salary (India): ₹7.5 LPA – ₹16.0 LPA
Job Description:
Ensures optimal inventory levels and accurate stock management across the organization.
Key Responsibilities:
• Develop inventory strategies and policies
• Monitor stock levels and turnover
• Reduce excess and obsolete inventory
• Conduct audits and reporting
• Collaborate with procurement and sales teams
Skills Required:
• Inventory optimization
• Data analysis
• ERP systems
