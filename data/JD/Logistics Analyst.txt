57. Logistics Analyst
Experience: 2–6 years
Salary (India): ₹4.5 LPA – ₹10.0 LPA
Job Description:
Analyzes logistics operations to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance service levels
using data-driven insights.
Key Responsibilities:
• Analyze transportation and warehouse data
• Identify inefficiencies and cost-saving opportunities
• Track KPIs such as delivery time and cost per shipment
• Prepare dashboards and reports
• Support logistics strategy and decision-making
Skills Required:
• Data analysis (Excel, SQL, Power BI/Tableau)
• Logistics process knowledge
• Problem-solving and analytical thinking
