22. Logistics Supervisor
Experience: 5–10 years
Salary (India): ₹5.5 LPA – ₹11.0 LPA
Job Description:
Supervises logistics teams and daily operations, ensuring smooth workflow and adherence to
company policies.
Key Responsibilities:
• Supervise logistics staff and operations
• Monitor shipment schedules and execution
• Ensure compliance with safety and regulatory standards
• Improve operational efficiency
• Handle team training and performance evaluation
Skills Required:
• Team management
• Operational control
• Process improvement
