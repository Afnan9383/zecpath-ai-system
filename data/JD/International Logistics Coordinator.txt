50. International Logistics Coordinator
Experience: 2–6 years
Salary (India): ₹4.0 LPA – ₹9.0 LPA
Job Description:
Coordinates international shipments, ensuring smooth movement of goods across borders
with proper documentation and compliance.
Key Responsibilities:
• Plan and coordinate international shipments (air/sea/road)
• Communicate with freight forwarders and carriers
• Track shipments and manage delivery timelines
• Prepare shipping and export/import documentation

• Handle Incoterms and international trade requirements
• Resolve shipment delays and issues
Skills Required:
• International shipping knowledge
• Understanding of Incoterms
• Coordination and communication skills
• ERP/logistics software
