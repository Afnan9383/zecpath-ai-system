16. Import/Export Coordinator
Experience: 2–5 years
Salary (India): ₹3.5 LPA – ₹7.5 LPA
Job Description:
Manages international logistics operations including customs clearance, documentation, and
compliance.
Key Responsibilities:
• Handle import/export documentation
• Coordinate with customs brokers and authorities
• Ensure compliance with trade regulations
• Track international shipments
• Manage duties, taxes, and shipping costs
Skills Required:
• Knowledge of international trade regulations
• Documentation (Bill of Lading, LC, etc.)
• Customs procedures
