69. Logistics & Finance Analyst
Experience: 3–8 years
Salary (India): ₹5.5 LPA – ₹13.0 LPA
Job Description:
Analyzes logistics costs, budgets, and financial performance to improve cost efficiency and
profitability.
Key Responsibilities:
• Analyze logistics and transportation costs
• Prepare budgets and financial reports
• Identify cost-saving opportunities
• Monitor freight and operational expenses
• Support financial planning and forecasting
Skills Required:
• Financial analysis and accounting basics
• Excel, BI tools, and reporting
• Cost optimization and analytical thinking
