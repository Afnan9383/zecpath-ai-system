7. Transport Coordinator
Experience: 0–2 years
Salary (India): ₹2.2 LPA – ₹4.2 LPA
Job Description:
Coordinates transportation operations to ensure timely delivery of goods.
Key Responsibilities:
• Plan vehicle allocation and routes
• Coordinate with drivers and transport vendors
• Track shipments in transit
• Optimize transportation costs
• Handle delivery issues
Skills Required:
• Route planning
• Vendor coordination
• Problem-solving skills
