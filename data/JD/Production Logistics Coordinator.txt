74. Production Logistics Coordinator
Experience: 2–6 years
Salary (India): ₹3.5 LPA – ₹8.5 LPA
Job Description:
Coordinates material flow within the production environment to ensure uninterrupted
manufacturing operations.
Key Responsibilities:
• Ensure timely availability of raw materials at production lines
• Coordinate with procurement, warehouse, and production teams
• Monitor material movement and consumption
• Manage line-side inventory and minimize shortages
• Support production planning and scheduling
• Maintain logistics and material movement records
Skills Required:
• Production and manufacturing logistics knowledge
• Inventory and material handling
• ERP systems (SAP preferred)
• Coordination and problem-solving skills
