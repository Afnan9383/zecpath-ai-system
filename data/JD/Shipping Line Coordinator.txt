55. Shipping Line Coordinator
Experience: 2–6 years
Salary (India): ₹4.0 LPA – ₹9.0 LPA
Job Description:
Coordinates with shipping lines for ocean freight operations, ensuring smooth booking,
documentation, and vessel scheduling.
Key Responsibilities:
• Coordinate vessel bookings and schedules
• Communicate with shipping lines and port authorities
• Prepare and manage shipping documentation
• Track shipments and manage delays
• Ensure compliance with port and shipping regulations
• Handle customer and internal coordination
Skills Required:
• Knowledge of ocean freight operations
• Documentation and compliance
• Coordination and communication skills
• Attention to detail
