2. Logistics Coordinator (Junior)
Experience: 0–2 years
Salary (India): ₹2.5 LPA – ₹4.5 LPA
Job Description:
Coordinates inbound and outbound logistics operations, ensuring timely delivery and proper
documentation.
Key Responsibilities:
• Plan and coordinate shipments
• Communicate with suppliers, transporters, and customers
• Monitor delivery timelines
• Handle documentation and compliance
• Resolve basic logistics issues
Skills Required:
• Logistics coordination skills
• Time management
• ERP systems (SAP preferred)
