45. Warehouse Operations Manager
Experience: 6–12 years
Salary (India): ₹8.0 LPA – ₹18.0 LPA
Job Description:
Oversees end-to-end warehouse operations, ensuring efficient storage, handling, and dispatch
of goods while maintaining safety and cost efficiency.
Key Responsibilities:
• Manage daily warehouse operations (receiving, storage, picking, packing, dispatch)
• Optimize warehouse layout and space utilization
• Ensure inventory accuracy and stock control
• Implement warehouse management systems (WMS)
• Maintain safety standards and compliance
• Lead and train warehouse staff
• Monitor KPIs such as order accuracy, turnaround time, and productivity
Skills Required:
• Warehouse management systems (WMS)
• Team leadership and workforce management
• Process optimization and lean practices
• Inventory control knowledge
