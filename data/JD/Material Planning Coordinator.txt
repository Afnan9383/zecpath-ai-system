76. Material Planning Coordinator
Experience: 2–6 years
Salary (India): ₹4.0 LPA – ₹9.0 LPA
Job Description:
Plans and coordinates material requirements to ensure smooth production without delays or
excess inventory.
Key Responsibilities:
• Plan material requirements based on production schedules
• Coordinate with procurement and suppliers
• Monitor inventory levels and reorder points
• Avoid stockouts and excess inventory
• Track material consumption and forecast demand
• Maintain planning reports and data
Skills Required:
• Material requirement planning (MRP)
• Inventory and demand planning
• Data analysis and forecasting
• ERP systems (SAP, Oracle)
