42. Route Planning Analyst
Experience: 3–7 years
Salary (India): ₹5.0 LPA – ₹12.0 LPA
Job Description:
Analyzes and designs optimal transportation routes to improve delivery efficiency and reduce
logistics costs.
Key Responsibilities:
• Develop and optimize delivery routes using data analytics
• Analyze traffic patterns, delivery schedules, and constraints
• Use routing software and GIS tools
• Reduce transportation time and fuel costs
• Support last-mile and distribution planning
• Generate performance and optimization reports
Skills Required:
• Data analysis and optimization techniques
• Knowledge of routing tools (GIS, TMS)
• Strong Excel and analytical skills
• Problem-solving mindset
