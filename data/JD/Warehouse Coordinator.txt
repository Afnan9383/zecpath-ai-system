5. Warehouse Coordinator
Experience: 0–2 years
Salary (India): ₹2.2 LPA – ₹4.0 LPA
Job Description:
Coordinates warehouse activities including inventory handling, storage, and dispatch support.
Key Responsibilities:
• Manage goods receipt and storage
• Coordinate picking, packing, and dispatch
• Monitor stock levels
• Ensure warehouse safety and organization
• Maintain inventory records
Skills Required:
• Warehouse operations knowledge
• Inventory tracking
• Organizational skills
