53. Freight Broker
Experience: 3–8 years
Salary (India): ₹5.0 LPA – ₹15.0 LPA (including incentives/commission)
Job Description:
Acts as an intermediary between shippers and carriers, arranging transportation of goods at
competitive rates while ensuring timely delivery.
Key Responsibilities:
• Identify and onboard shippers and carriers
• Negotiate freight rates and contracts
• Arrange transportation and track shipments
• Manage load matching and capacity planning
• Resolve shipment issues and delays
• Maintain relationships with clients and carriers
Skills Required:
• Strong negotiation and sales skills
• Knowledge of freight markets and pricing
• Communication and relationship management
• Problem-solving and decision-making
