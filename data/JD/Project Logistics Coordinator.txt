80. Project Logistics Coordinator
Experience: 3–8 years
Salary (India): ₹5.0 LPA – ₹14.0 LPA
Job Description:
Coordinates logistics activities for large-scale industrial or infrastructure projects, ensuring
timely delivery of materials, equipment, and resources to project sites.
Key Responsibilities:
• Plan and coordinate project logistics from origin to site
• Manage transportation of heavy and critical materials
• Coordinate with vendors, contractors, and project teams
• Track shipments and ensure on-time delivery
• Handle project documentation and compliance
• Identify and mitigate logistics risks
Skills Required:
• Project logistics and coordination
• Knowledge of heavy cargo handling
• Vendor and stakeholder management
• Problem-solving and planning skills
