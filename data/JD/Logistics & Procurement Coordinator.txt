65. Logistics & Procurement Coordinator
Experience: 2–6 years
Salary (India): ₹4.5 LPA – ₹10.0 LPA
Job Description:
Bridges procurement and logistics functions to ensure timely sourcing and delivery of
materials.
Key Responsibilities:
• Coordinate procurement and transportation activities
• Manage supplier communication and delivery schedules
• Track purchase orders and shipments
• Ensure timely material availability
• Optimize procurement and logistics costs
Skills Required:
• Procurement and sourcing knowledge
• Vendor management
• Negotiation and coordination skills
