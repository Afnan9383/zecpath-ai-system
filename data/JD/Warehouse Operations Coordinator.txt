18. Warehouse Operations Coordinator
Experience: 2–5 years
Salary (India): ₹3.5 LPA – ₹6.5 LPA
Job Description:
Coordinates daily warehouse operations including receiving, storage, picking, packing, and
dispatch.
Key Responsibilities:
• Supervise warehouse activities
• Ensure efficient space utilization
• Coordinate picking and packing processes
• Maintain safety and quality standards
• Monitor warehouse KPIs
Skills Required:
• Warehouse management systems (WMS)
• Process improvement
• Team coordination
