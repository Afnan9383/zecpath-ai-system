82. Offshore Logistics Coordinator
Experience: 3–10 years
Salary (India): ₹6.0 LPA – ₹18.0 LPA (can be higher offshore/international)
Job Description:
Coordinates logistics operations for offshore facilities such as oil rigs, ensuring timely
delivery of equipment, supplies, and personnel.
Key Responsibilities:
• Plan and coordinate offshore supply operations
• Manage vessel and helicopter logistics
• Ensure timely delivery of materials to offshore platforms
• Coordinate with offshore teams and onshore bases
• Maintain safety and regulatory compliance
• Handle emergency logistics requirements
Skills Required:
• Offshore logistics and marine operations knowledge
• Coordination with vessels and aviation logistics
• Safety and compliance awareness
• Strong communication and problem-solving skills
