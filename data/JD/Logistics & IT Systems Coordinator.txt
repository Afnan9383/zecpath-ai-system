70. Logistics & IT Systems Coordinator
Experience: 3–8 years
Salary (India): ₹5.5 LPA – ₹14.0 LPA
Job Description:
Manages logistics-related IT systems and ensures smooth integration between logistics
operations and technology platforms.
Key Responsibilities:
• Maintain and support logistics systems (ERP, WMS, TMS)
• Coordinate system upgrades and implementations
• Troubleshoot system issues
• Ensure data accuracy and system integration
• Train users on logistics software
Skills Required:
• ERP/WMS/TMS systems knowledge
• Basic IT and system integration skills
• Problem-solving and analytical skills
