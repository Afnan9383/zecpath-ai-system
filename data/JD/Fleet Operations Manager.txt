41. Fleet Operations Manager
Experience: 6–12 years
Salary (India): ₹8.0 LPA – ₹18.0 LPA
Job Description:
Oversees end-to-end fleet operations, ensuring optimal utilization, maintenance, compliance,
and cost efficiency of vehicles.
Key Responsibilities:
• Manage daily fleet operations and vehicle allocation
• Monitor fleet performance, fuel consumption, and maintenance schedules
• Ensure compliance with transport regulations and safety standards
• Optimize fleet utilization and reduce operational costs
• Coordinate with drivers, vendors, and internal teams
• Implement fleet management systems and telematics
Skills Required:
• Fleet management and vehicle operations
• Cost control and budgeting
• Leadership and vendor management
• Knowledge of GPS/telematics systems
