61. Inventory Planning Analyst
Experience: 2–7 years
Salary (India): ₹5.0 LPA – ₹12.0 LPA
Job Description:
Plans and manages inventory levels to ensure product availability while minimizing costs.
Key Responsibilities:
• Develop inventory plans and policies
• Monitor stock levels and turnover
• Optimize safety stock and reorder points
• Analyze slow-moving and obsolete inventory
• Collaborate with procurement and logistics teams
Skills Required:
• Inventory planning techniques
• Data analysis and forecasting
• ERP and inventory management systems
