47. Stock Control Manager
Experience: 6–12 years
Salary (India): ₹7.0 LPA – ₹16.0 LPA
Job Description:
Responsible for maintaining accurate stock levels, controlling inventory movements, and
minimizing losses.
Key Responsibilities:
• Develop stock control policies and procedures
• Monitor stock levels and inventory accuracy
• Conduct audits and cycle counts
• Investigate discrepancies and implement corrective actions
• Coordinate with warehouse and procurement teams
• Ensure compliance with inventory standards
Skills Required:
• Inventory control systems
• Audit and compliance knowledge
• Analytical and reporting skills
• Attention to detail
