11. Logistics Coordinator
Experience: 2–5 years
Salary (India): ₹3.5 LPA – ₹7.0 LPA
Job Description:
Manages end-to-end logistics operations including planning, execution, and monitoring of
shipments to ensure timely delivery and cost efficiency.
Key Responsibilities:
• Plan and coordinate inbound and outbound shipments
• Track shipments and resolve delays
• Coordinate with vendors, transporters, and warehouses
• Maintain logistics documentation and reports
• Optimize logistics costs and delivery timelines
Skills Required:
• Logistics operations management
• ERP systems (SAP, Oracle)
• Vendor coordination and problem-solving
