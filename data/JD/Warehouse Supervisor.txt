25. Warehouse Supervisor
Experience: 5–10 years
Salary (India): ₹5.0 LPA – ₹10.0 LPA
Job Description:
Supervises warehouse operations including storage, inventory handling, and dispatch
activities.
Key Responsibilities:
• Manage warehouse staff and operations
• Ensure proper storage and inventory accuracy
• Oversee picking, packing, and dispatch
• Maintain safety and compliance standards
• Improve warehouse efficiency
Skills Required:
• Warehouse management systems (WMS)
• Team leadership
• Process optimization
