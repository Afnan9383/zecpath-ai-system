59. Demand Planning Analyst
Experience: 2–7 years
Salary (India): ₹5.5 LPA – ₹13.0 LPA
Job Description:
Forecasts customer demand to ensure optimal inventory levels and avoid stockouts or
overstocking.
Key Responsibilities:
• Develop demand forecasts using historical data
• Collaborate with sales and marketing teams
• Analyze trends and seasonal variations
• Adjust forecasts based on market conditions
• Improve forecast accuracy
Skills Required:
• Forecasting techniques
• Statistical analysis
• Excel, Python, or forecasting tools
