77. Cold Chain Logistics Coordinator
Experience: 2–6 years
Salary (India): ₹4.5 LPA – ₹10.0 LPA
Job Description:
Manages temperature-controlled logistics operations for sensitive products such as vaccines,
biologics, and perishable pharmaceuticals.
Key Responsibilities:
• Coordinate temperature-controlled shipments (2°C–8°C, frozen, ambient)
• Monitor real-time temperature data using tracking systems
• Ensure compliance with cold chain standards and regulations
• Coordinate with transporters and cold storage facilities
• Handle deviations and temperature excursions
• Maintain documentation and audit records

Skills Required:
• Cold chain logistics knowledge
• Understanding of GDP (Good Distribution Practices)
• Monitoring tools and IoT-based tracking systems
• Attention to detail and compliance focus
