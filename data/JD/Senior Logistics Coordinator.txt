21. Senior Logistics Coordinator
Experience: 5–8 years
Salary (India): ₹5.0 LPA – ₹10.0 LPA
Job Description:
Leads and oversees complex logistics operations, ensuring efficient coordination of
shipments, vendors, and internal teams.
Key Responsibilities:
• Manage end-to-end logistics operations
• Supervise junior coordinators
• Optimize delivery timelines and costs
• Resolve escalations and operational issues
• Generate performance and KPI reports
Skills Required:
• Advanced logistics planning
• ERP/WMS systems expertise
• Leadership and problem-solving
