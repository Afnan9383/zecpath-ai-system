71. E-commerce Logistics Coordinator
Experience: 2–5 years
Salary (India): ₹3.5 LPA – ₹8.0 LPA
Job Description:
Manages logistics operations for e-commerce businesses, ensuring smooth order processing,
inventory movement, and timely deliveries.
Key Responsibilities:
• Coordinate order fulfillment from warehouse to customer
• Manage courier and delivery partner relationships
• Track shipments and resolve delivery issues
• Monitor return (RTO) and reverse logistics processes
• Ensure inventory availability and order accuracy
• Maintain logistics dashboards and reports
Skills Required:
• E-commerce logistics and order management systems
• Knowledge of last-mile delivery processes
• Coordination and problem-solving skills
• Familiarity with platforms and OMS tools
