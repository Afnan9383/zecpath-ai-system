62. Operations Analyst
Experience: 2–6 years
Salary (India): ₹4.5 LPA – ₹10.0 LPA
Job Description:
Analyzes operational processes to improve efficiency, productivity, and cost-effectiveness
across logistics and supply chain functions.
Key Responsibilities:
• Evaluate operational workflows and processes
• Identify inefficiencies and improvement areas
• Develop performance metrics and KPIs
• Support automation and process improvement initiatives
• Generate reports and dashboards
Skills Required:
• Process analysis and optimization
• Data analytics tools
• Problem-solving and critical thinking
