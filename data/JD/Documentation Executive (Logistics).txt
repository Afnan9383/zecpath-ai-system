10. Documentation Executive (Logistics)
Experience: 0–2 years
Salary (India): ₹2.0 LPA – ₹3.8 LPA
Job Description:
Manages logistics documentation ensuring compliance with regulatory and company
standards.
Key Responsibilities:
• Prepare invoices, packing lists, and shipping documents
• Handle import/export documentation
• Ensure compliance with regulations
• Maintain records and filing systems
• Coordinate with customs and logistics teams
Skills Required:
• Documentation accuracy
• Knowledge of logistics paperwork
• Attention to detail
