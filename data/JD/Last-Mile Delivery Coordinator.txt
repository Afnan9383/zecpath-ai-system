43. Last-Mile Delivery Coordinator
Experience: 2–6 years
Salary (India): ₹3.5 LPA – ₹8.0 LPA
Job Description:
Manages last-mile delivery operations, ensuring timely and accurate delivery to end
customers.
Key Responsibilities:
• Coordinate final delivery operations and schedules
• Assign deliveries to drivers or delivery partners
• Monitor real-time delivery tracking
• Handle customer queries and delivery issues
• Improve delivery speed and customer satisfaction
• Ensure proof of delivery and documentation
Skills Required:
• Last-mile logistics knowledge
• Real-time tracking tools
• Customer handling and coordination
• Problem-solving under pressure
