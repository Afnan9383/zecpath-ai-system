54. Freight Analyst
Experience: 2–6 years
Salary (India): ₹4.5 LPA – ₹10.0 LPA

Job Description:
Analyzes freight operations and costs to improve efficiency, reduce expenses, and optimize
transportation strategies.
Key Responsibilities:
• Analyze freight cost data and trends
• Identify cost-saving opportunities
• Evaluate carrier performance and contracts
• Develop freight optimization strategies
• Generate reports and dashboards
• Support logistics and procurement teams
Skills Required:
• Data analysis and reporting
• Advanced Excel, SQL, or BI tools
• Understanding of freight operations
• Analytical and problem-solving skills
