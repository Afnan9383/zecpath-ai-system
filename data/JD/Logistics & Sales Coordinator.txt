68. Logistics & Sales Coordinator
Experience: 2–6 years
Salary (India): ₹4.0 LPA – ₹9.0 LPA
Job Description:
Supports sales teams by ensuring product availability, timely delivery, and coordination of
logistics activities.
Key Responsibilities:
• Coordinate order processing and delivery schedules
• Communicate with sales and logistics teams
• Ensure timely fulfillment of customer orders
• Track shipments and resolve delivery issues
• Support sales forecasting and planning
Skills Required:
• Sales coordination
• Logistics and order management
• Communication and planning skills
