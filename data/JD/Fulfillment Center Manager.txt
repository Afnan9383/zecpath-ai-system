48. Fulfillment Center Manager
Experience: 6–12 years
Salary (India): ₹8.0 LPA – ₹20.0 LPA
Job Description:
Manages operations of fulfillment centers, ensuring efficient order processing, packaging,
and delivery in high-volume environments (especially e-commerce).
Key Responsibilities:
• Oversee end-to-end fulfillment operations
• Manage order processing, picking, packing, and shipping
• Ensure high order accuracy and on-time delivery
• Optimize workforce productivity and automation
• Implement process improvements and technology solutions
• Monitor customer satisfaction and service levels

Skills Required:
• Fulfillment and e-commerce operations
• Warehouse automation systems
• Team leadership and performance management
• Process improvement and KPI management
