49. Customs Clearance Specialist
Experience: 3–8 years
Salary (India): ₹4.5 LPA – ₹10.0 LPA
Job Description:
Handles customs clearance processes for import and export shipments, ensuring compliance
with government regulations and timely clearance of goods.
Key Responsibilities:
• Prepare and submit customs documentation
• Coordinate with customs brokers and authorities
• Classify goods using HS codes
• Calculate duties, taxes, and tariffs
• Resolve customs-related issues and delays
• Ensure compliance with import/export regulations
Skills Required:
• Knowledge of customs regulations and procedures
• HS classification and tariff understanding
• Documentation and compliance expertise
• Attention to detail
