23. Transportation Supervisor
Experience: 5–10 years
Salary (India): ₹5.5 LPA – ₹10.5 LPA
Job Description:
Manages transportation operations including route planning, fleet management, and delivery
performance.
Key Responsibilities:
• Oversee transportation scheduling and routing
• Manage fleet utilization and driver coordination
• Monitor delivery timelines and costs
• Resolve transportation issues and delays
• Ensure compliance with transport regulations
Skills Required:
• Route optimization
• Fleet management
• Decision-making under pressure
