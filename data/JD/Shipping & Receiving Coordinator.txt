14. Shipping & Receiving Coordinator
Experience: 2–5 years
Salary (India): ₹3.0 LPA – ₹6.0 LPA
Job Description:
Oversees inbound (receiving) and outbound (shipping) operations ensuring accuracy and
compliance.
Key Responsibilities:
• Manage receiving and shipping processes
• Verify incoming and outgoing shipments
• Maintain records of shipments
• Coordinate with suppliers and customers
• Ensure compliance with safety and quality standards
Skills Required:
• Warehouse and shipping knowledge
• Documentation accuracy
• Attention to detail
