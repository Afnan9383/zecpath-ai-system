20. 3PL (Third-Party Logistics) Coordinator
Experience: 2–5 years
Salary (India): ₹3.5 LPA – ₹7.5 LPA
Job Description:
Acts as a liaison between the company and third-party logistics providers to ensure smooth
logistics operations.
Key Responsibilities:
• Coordinate with 3PL vendors for warehousing and transportation
• Monitor service levels and performance
• Manage contracts and SLAs
• Resolve operational issues
• Optimize logistics costs and efficiency
Skills Required:
• Vendor management
• Contract and SLA understanding
• Analytical and coordination skills
