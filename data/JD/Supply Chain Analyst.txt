58. Supply Chain Analyst
Experience: 2–6 years
Salary (India): ₹5.0 LPA – ₹12.0 LPA
Job Description:
Evaluates end-to-end supply chain performance and recommends improvements across
procurement, production, and distribution.
Key Responsibilities:
• Analyze supply chain data and performance metrics
• Identify bottlenecks and inefficiencies
• Support demand planning and inventory strategies
• Collaborate with cross-functional teams
• Develop reports and predictive models
Skills Required:
• Supply chain knowledge
• Data analytics and forecasting
• ERP systems (SAP, Oracle)
