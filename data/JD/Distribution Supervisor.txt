24. Distribution Supervisor

Salary (India): ₹5.5 LPA – ₹10.5 LPA

distribution centers.

• Optimize distribution routes and costs
• Ensure customer satisfaction
Skills Required:
• Distribution management
• Analytical thinking
• Coordination skills
