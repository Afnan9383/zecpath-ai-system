9. Supply Chain Assistant
Experience: 0–2 years
Salary (India): ₹2.5 LPA – ₹4.5 LPA
Job Description:
Provides support across supply chain functions including procurement, logistics, and
inventory.
Key Responsibilities:
• Assist in demand planning
• Support procurement activities
• Coordinate logistics operations
• Maintain supply chain data
• Generate reports
Skills Required:
• Basic supply chain knowledge
• Analytical skills
• Excel and reporting
