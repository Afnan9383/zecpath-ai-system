13. Distribution Coordinator
Experience: 2–5 years
Salary (India): ₹3.5 LPA – ₹6.5 LPA
Job Description:
Coordinates distribution activities to ensure efficient delivery of goods from warehouses to
customers or retail outlets.
Key Responsibilities:
• Plan distribution schedules
• Coordinate with warehouses and transport teams
• Monitor delivery performance
• Optimize distribution routes
• Maintain distribution reports
Skills Required:
• Distribution planning
• Route optimization
• Coordination and analytical skills
