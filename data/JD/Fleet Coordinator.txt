19. Fleet Coordinator
Experience: 2–5 years
Salary (India): ₹3.0 LPA – ₹6.5 LPA
Job Description:
Manages company or vendor fleet operations ensuring efficient vehicle utilization and
maintenance.
Key Responsibilities:
• Monitor vehicle schedules and availability
• Coordinate maintenance and repairs
• Track fuel usage and costs
• Ensure compliance with regulations
• Optimize fleet utilization
Skills Required:
• Fleet management systems
• Cost control
• Coordination and planning
