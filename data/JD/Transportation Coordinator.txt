15. Transportation Coordinator
Experience: 2–5 years
Salary (India): ₹3.5 LPA – ₹7.0 LPA
Job Description:
Plans and manages transportation activities including route planning, fleet utilization, and
delivery tracking.
Key Responsibilities:
• Plan transportation routes and schedules
• Coordinate with drivers and transport vendors
• Monitor shipment progress
• Optimize transportation costs
• Handle delivery issues and escalations
Skills Required:
• Route planning and optimization
• Fleet coordination
• Problem-solving and decision-making
