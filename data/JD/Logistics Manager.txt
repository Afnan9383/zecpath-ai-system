31. Logistics Manager
Experience: 8–12 years
Salary (India): ₹8.0 LPA – ₹18.0 LPA
Job Description:
Oversees end-to-end logistics operations including transportation, warehousing, and
distribution to ensure efficiency and cost control.
Key Responsibilities:
• Develop logistics strategies and policies
• Manage transportation, warehousing, and distribution
• Optimize logistics costs and service levels
• Lead cross-functional teams
• Monitor KPIs and performance metrics
Skills Required:
• Strategic planning
• Leadership and team management
• ERP/WMS systems expertise
