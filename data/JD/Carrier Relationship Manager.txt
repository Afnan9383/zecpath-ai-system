44. Carrier Relationship Manager
Experience: 5–10 years
Salary (India): ₹6.0 LPA – ₹15.0 LPA
Job Description:
Manages relationships with transportation carriers and logistics vendors to ensure service
quality and cost efficiency.
Key Responsibilities:
• Develop and maintain relationships with carriers and transport vendors
• Negotiate contracts, rates, and service agreements
• Monitor carrier performance and KPIs
• Resolve service issues and escalations
• Ensure compliance with SLAs and regulatory requirements
• Identify and onboard new carriers
Skills Required:
• Vendor and relationship management
• Negotiation and contract management
• Performance analysis
• Communication and stakeholder management
