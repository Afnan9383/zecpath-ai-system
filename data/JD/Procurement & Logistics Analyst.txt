63. Procurement & Logistics Analyst
Experience: 3–8 years
Salary (India): ₹5.5 LPA – ₹13.0 LPA
Job Description:
Analyzes procurement and logistics data to optimize sourcing, supplier performance, and
transportation efficiency.
Key Responsibilities:
• Analyze supplier performance and procurement costs
• Optimize sourcing strategies
• Monitor logistics costs and performance
• Support contract negotiations
• Develop reports and insights
Skills Required:
• Procurement and logistics knowledge
• Data analysis and reporting
• Negotiation and cost analysis
