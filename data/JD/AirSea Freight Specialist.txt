56. Air/Sea Freight Specialist
Experience: 3–8 years
Salary (India): ₹5.0 LPA – ₹12.0 LPA
Job Description:
Manages air and sea freight operations including shipment planning, carrier coordination, and
documentation.
Key Responsibilities:
• Plan and execute air and sea shipments
• Coordinate with airlines, shipping lines, and freight forwarders
• Prepare and verify shipping documents
• Track shipments and ensure timely delivery
• Handle customs clearance coordination
• Optimize freight costs and transit times
Skills Required:
• Air and ocean freight expertise
• Knowledge of international shipping regulations
• Documentation and compliance
• Vendor coordination and negotiation
