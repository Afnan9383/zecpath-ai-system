3. Dispatch Coordinator
Experience: 0–2 years
Experience: 0–2 years
Salary (India): ₹2.0 LPA – ₹3.8 LPA
Job Description:
Manages dispatch operations, ensuring timely shipment of goods from warehouse to
customers.
Key Responsibilities:
• Schedule dispatches and deliveries
• Prepare dispatch documentation (invoice, packing list)
• Coordinate with warehouse and drivers
• Track dispatch status
• Ensure delivery accuracy
Skills Required:
• Dispatch planning
• Documentation accuracy
• Coordination skills
