1. Logistics Assistant
Experience: 0–2 years
Salary (India): ₹1.8 LPA – ₹3.5 LPA
Job Description:
Supports daily logistics operations including shipment tracking, coordination with vendors,
and documentation. Works closely with warehouse and transport teams to ensure smooth
material flow.
Key Responsibilities:
• Assist in shipment planning and scheduling
• Track deliveries and update status reports
• Coordinate with transporters and vendors
• Maintain logistics records and documentation
• Support inventory and warehouse activities
Skills Required:
• Basic knowledge of logistics processes
• MS Excel and ERP basics
• Communication and coordination skills
