52. Export Documentation Specialist
Experience: 2–6 years
Salary (India): ₹3.5 LPA – ₹8.0 LPA
Job Description:
Prepares and manages export documentation required for international shipments, ensuring
accuracy and compliance.
Key Responsibilities:
• Prepare export documents (Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading, Certificate of Origin)

• Coordinate with banks for Letter of Credit (LC) documentation
• Ensure compliance with export regulations
• Maintain documentation records and filing systems
• Coordinate with logistics and customs teams
• Resolve documentation discrepancies
Skills Required:
• Export documentation knowledge
• Attention to detail
• Knowledge of LC and banking procedures
• Communication and coordination skills
