30. Dispatch Manager
Experience: 5–10 years
Salary (India): ₹5.5 LPA – ₹11.5 LPA
Job Description:
Manages dispatch operations ensuring timely and accurate delivery of goods.
Key Responsibilities:
• Plan and oversee dispatch schedules
• Coordinate with warehouse and transport teams
• Monitor delivery timelines
• Ensure accuracy of dispatch documentation
• Optimize dispatch efficiency
Skills Required:
• Dispatch planning
• Coordination and supervision
• Problem-solving
