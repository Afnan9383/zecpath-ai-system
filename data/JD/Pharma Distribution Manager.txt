79. Pharma Distribution Manager
Experience: 6–12 years
Salary (India): ₹8.0 LPA – ₹20.0 LPA
Job Description:
Oversees pharmaceutical distribution networks ensuring safe, compliant, and efficient
delivery of medicines to distributors, hospitals, and pharmacies.
Key Responsibilities:
• Manage pharma distribution channels and partners
• Ensure compliance with regulatory standards (GDP, drug regulations)
• Monitor product handling, storage, and transportation
• Optimize distribution network efficiency
• Manage inventory across distribution points

• Handle audits, compliance checks, and quality assurance
Skills Required:
• Pharmaceutical logistics and compliance expertise
• Distribution network management
• Leadership and team management
• Regulatory knowledge and audit handling
